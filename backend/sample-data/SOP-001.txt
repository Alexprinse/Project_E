STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: SOP-001
Title: C-101 Column and P-101A Bottoms Pump Startup
Location: Refinery Unit 3
Effective Date: 2026-07-20

1. PURPOSE
This procedure outlines the safe startup sequence for the C-101 distillation column and its associated bottoms pump, P-101A.

2. PRE-STARTUP CHECKS
- Verify all maintenance work is complete and blinds are removed.
- Ensure the C-101 column base liquid level is established at >30%.
- Confirm suction and discharge valves for P-101A are in the correct open/closed positions.

3. STARTUP PROCEDURE
3.1. Field Operator: Crack open the P-101A suction valve to prime the pump and clear any vapor locks.
3.2. Board Operator: Verify the DCS motor run indication for P-101A is ready.
3.3. Board Operator: Start P-101A from the DCS.
3.4. Field Operator: Slowly open the discharge valve to establish flow.
3.5. Board Operator: Monitor the discharge pressure.
   - **Target:** The normal discharge pressure target for P-101A is 450 psi.
   - **Safety Limit:** Do not exceed the safety interlock threshold of 500 psi. If pressure approaches this limit, the interlock will automatically trip the pump to prevent mechanical failure.

4. POST-STARTUP
- Verify steady-state operation and check for any abnormal vibrations or seal leaks.
- Log the successful startup and any pressure deviations in the shift handover report.
